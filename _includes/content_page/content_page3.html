English Words 3 Turkish Meaning Spelling Sentence
since o zamandan beri sîns since is used to talk about a past event still happening now.
visible görülebilir vîzıbıl If something is visible it can be seen.
wild vahşi wayld If something is wild it is found in nature.
advantage avantaj ädväntîc An advantage is something that helps you.
cause neden olmak kôz To cause is to make something happen.
choice seçme çoys A choice is the act or possibility of picking something.
community halk kımyunıti A community is a group of people who live together.
dead ölü ded To be dead is to not be alive.
distance mesafe dîstıns The distance between two things is how far it is between them.
escape -den kaçmak îskeyp To escape is to run away from something bad.
face karşılamak feys If you face a problem you deal with it.
follow izlemek, peşinden gitmek fälō To follow means to go behind someone and go where they go.
fright korku frayt fright is the feeling of being scared.
ghost hayalet, hortlak gōst A ghost is the spirit of a dead person.
individual bireysel, özel îndıvîcıwıl An individual is one person.
pet evde beslenen hayvan pet A pet is an animal that lives with people.
reach uzanmak, ulaşmak riç To reach means to arrive at a place.
return dönmek, geri gelmek ritırn To return is to go back to a place.
survive hayatta kalmak sırvayv To survive is to stay alive. (despite some danger or illness)
upset üzgün ıpset To be upset is to be unhappy about something.
voice ses voys A voice is the sound a person makes when they talk or sing.
weather hava wedhır The weather is the condition of the air: hot, rainy, windy etc.
wise akıllı, mantıklı wayz To be wise is to use experience and intelligence to make good choices.
allow izin vermek ılau To allow something to happen means to let it happen.
announce anons etmek, yüksek sesle bildirmek ınauns To announce something is to make it known.
beside yanında bîsayd When someone or something is beside you they are next to you.
challenge uğraştırıcı şey çälınc A challenge is something difficult to complete.
claim iddia etmek kleym To claim means to say that something is true.
condition içinde bulunulan durum, koşul kındîşın The condition of someone or something is the state that they are in.
contribute katkıda bulunmak kıntrîbyut To contribute to something means to do something to make it successful.
difference fark, ayrım dîfrıns A difference is a way that something is not like other things.
divide bölmek, ayırmak dîvayd To divide something is to make it into smaller parts.
expert uzman, bilirkişi ekspırt An expert is someone who is very good at doing something.
famous ünlü, meşhur feymıs If someone or something is famous they are known to many people.
force güç, kuvvet, zor fôrs force is a person's strength or power.
harm zarar, ziyan, hasar härm harm is hurt or problems caused to someone or something.
lay yaymak, sermek ley To lay means to put or place in a horizontal or flat position.
peace barış pis peace is a time without war.
prince prens prîns A prince is the son of a king.
protect korumak prıtekt To protect someone is to stop them from getting hurt.
sense sezmek sens To sense something is to know about it without being told.
sudden ani sʌdın When something is sudden it happens very quickly.
therefore bu nedenle dherfôr therefore means for this reason.
accept kabul etmek äksept To accept something that is offered is to take it.
arrange düzenli bir şekilde yerleştirmek ıreync To arrange things is to put them in the right place.
attend dikkatini vermek, (iş, toplantı) katılmak ıtend To attend something is to go to it.
balance dengelemek bälıns To balance something is to keep it from falling.
contrast karşıtlık, tezat kınträst A contrast is the sharp difference between two things.
encourage yüreklendirmek enkırîc To encourage someone is to make them want to do something.
